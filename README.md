# 🛍️ Store Sales Analysis Dashboard (Excel)

## 📌 Project Overview

The Store Sales Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Microsoft Excel dashboard developed to analyze annual sales performance, customer demographics, order trends, and product categories. The dashboard provides business insights through charts, Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, Slicers, and KPIs, helping stakeholders make data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objective

- Analyze annual store sales performance.
- Monitor monthly sales and order trends.
- Identify top-performing states and sales channels.
- Understand customer demographics based on age and gender.
- Analyze order status and product category performance.
- Support business decision-making through interactive Excel dashboards.

- Order Date
- Sales Amount
- Quantity

## 📊 Dashboard Features

- 📅 Monthly Orders vs Sales Analysis
- 👨‍👩‍👧 Sales by Age & Gender
- 📦 Order Status Distribution
- 🗺️ Top 5 States by Sales
- 🛍️ Sales by Men vs Women
- 🎛️ Interactive Slicers
  - Month
  - Sales Channel
  - Product Category

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- Women contribute approximately **64%** of total sales.
- The **Delivered** order status accounts for the majority of orders (**92%**).
- **Maharashtra** is the highest revenue-generating state.
- Adult customers contribute the highest percentage of total sales.
- Monthly sales trend helps identify peak and low-performing months.
- Interactive filters allow users to analyze sales dynamically.
